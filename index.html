Woman Found Dead under vermont's burning Ash

Edited by: Simon Reinhardt, 11th April 1995

Police identified the body dicovered on a burning property near the deerfield river, is that of the vermont woman reported missing almost four weeks ago.
Stacy Heine, 28, was last seen by her friends as they were working on a project concerning narcotics deemed revolutionary in terms of lengthening life expectancy.

At the time, Heine left their office in a state her friends describe as 'disturbing'.

The investigation into Stacy heine's death hasn't led to any arrests as of yet.
Police didn't give any indications to who might have slit her throat and burned her body. Though the evidence had been lost in the fire, and no footmarks were found at the scene besides Stacy's.
